2 | Review list from Happy Souper


Limitations of database

  • Source of Data
    • Data are not official and many terms are not defined.
    • The dataset relies on personal preference, it may not be subjective enough, but still can be referenced and compared with the popularities defined by marketsales.
    • Not all the ramens on market is included, thus may be not comprehensive enough.
    • The different tastes (Sweet, sour, spicy etc.) can be categorized to give a better reference to readers.
  • DV
    • Due to the limitation with data and R programming skills, the DV types and styles is limited.
    • More interactive DV can be made.
    • More detailed information can be added to avoid creating overlapping DV.


Future insights

  • We may reference to the DV to predict the place that has a good over-all ramen quality
  • By mapping with the marketsales, we can indentify the companies that would have a greater influence on the ramens globally
