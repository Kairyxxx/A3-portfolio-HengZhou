Two datasets were used in this portfolio

1 | The big list
This dataset is derived from Kaggle - Ramen Ratings. THE RAMEN RATER has full credit to this dataset.
2 | Review list from Happy Souper


Limitations of database

  • Source of Data
    • Data are not official and many terms are not defined.
    • The dataset relies on personal preference, it may not be subjective enough, but still can be referenced and compared with the popularities defined by market sales.
    • Not all the ramen on market is included, thus maybe not be comprehensive enough.
    • The different tastes (Sweet, sour, spicy, etc.) can be categorized to give a better reference to readers.
  • DV
    • Due to the limitation of data and R programming skills, the DV types and styles are limited.
    • More interactive DV can be made.
    • More detailed information can be added to avoid creating overlapping DV.


Future insights

  • We may reference the DV to predict the place that has a good overall ramen quality
  • By mapping with the market sale, we can identify the companies that would have a greater influence on the ramens globally
